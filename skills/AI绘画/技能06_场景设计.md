## TL;DR

场景设计 = 场景类型 + 环境细节 + 光影设计 + 氛围营造 + 构图设计

## 1. 场景类型

### 1.1 自然场景
```
自然场景类型：

山地景观：
├── 高山：snow-capped peaks, alpine meadow
├── 丘陵：rolling hills, green valleys
├── 峡谷：deep canyon, rocky cliffs
├── 火山：volcanic landscape, crater lake
└── 沙漠：desert dunes, oasis, sandstorm

水域景观：
├── 海洋：vast ocean, turquoise waters
├── 湖泊：crystal lake, mountain lake
├── 河流：flowing river, waterfall
├── 海滩：sandy beach, rocky shore
└── 海岛：tropical island, coral reef

森林场景：
├── 热带雨林：lush rainforest, dense vegetation
├── 针叶林：coniferous forest, pine trees
├── 阔叶林：deciduous forest, autumn leaves
├── 竹林：bamboo grove, zen garden
└── 魔法森林：enchanted forest, glowing mushrooms

草原场景：
├── 大草原：vast savanna, golden grass
├── 高山草甸：alpine meadow, wildflowers
├── 湿地：wetland, marsh, reed beds
├── 苔原：arctic tundra, frozen landscape
└── 农田：farmland, rice paddies, vineyard

天文场景：
├── 星空：starry night, milky way
├── 日出日落：sunrise, golden sunset
├── 月夜：moonlit scene, full moon
├── 极光：aurora borealis, northern lights
└── 云海：sea of clouds, mountain peaks
```

## 4. 构图设计

### 4.1 构图方式
```
构图方式设计：

基本构图：
├── 中心构图：centered composition
├── 三分构图：rule of thirds
├── 对称构图：symmetrical composition
├── 对角构图：diagonal composition
└── 框架构图：framing composition

景别构图：
├── 远景：wide shot, establishing shot
├── 全景：panoramic view, full view
├── 中景：medium shot, waist level
├── 近景：close-up, chest level
├── 特写：extreme close-up, detail
└── 大特写：macro shot, tiny detail

视角构图：
├── 平视：eye level, neutral angle
├── 俯视：bird's eye view, overhead
├── 仰视：worm's eye view, looking up
├── 鸟瞰：aerial view, top-down
├── 倾斜：tilted, Dutch angle
└── 鱼眼：fisheye, curved perspective

特殊构图：
├── 引导线：leading lines
├── 留白：negative space
├── 层次：layered composition
├── 黄金分割：golden ratio
├── 螺旋构图：spiral composition
└── 三角构图：triangular composition
```

### 4.2 构图描述
```
构图描述词：

构图类型：
├── 全景展示：panoramic view, sweeping vista
├── 局部特写：intimate close-up, detail shot
├── 纵深感：depth of field, layered foreground
├── 平面感：flat composition, graphic style
├── 透视线：perspective lines, vanishing point
└── 框中框：frame within frame, window shot

景深控制：
├── 深景深：deep focus, everything sharp
├── 浅景深：shallow depth, bokeh background
├── 前景虚化：foreground blur
├── 背景虚化：background blur, bokeh
├── 散景效果：bokeh effect, dreamy
└── 全景清晰：everything in focus

画面元素：
├── 前景元素：foreground elements
├── 中景主体：middle ground subject
├── 背景环境：background environment
├── 留白空间：negative space, breathing room
├── 视觉引导：visual flow, leading eye
└── 平衡构图：balanced composition

视角描述：
├── 平视取景：eye-level perspective
├── 低角度仰拍：low angle, heroic
├── 高角度俯拍：high angle, vulnerable
├── 倾斜视角：tilted frame, unstable
├── 鸟瞰视角：bird's eye, overview
└── 第一人称：first person POV
```
